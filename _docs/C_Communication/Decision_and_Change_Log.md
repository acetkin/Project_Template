# Decision and Change Log
This document records all approved decisions that change direction, scope, constraints, or assumptions.
It is append-only and must never be rewritten or reordered.

## Logging Rules
- Only approved decisions may be logged.
- Each decision must be logged immediately after approval.
- Entries must not be edited or removed once written.
- Context_Packet.md must be updated after logging a decision.

## Decision Entry Format
Each entry MUST follow this structure exactly.

### Decision ID
A unique incremental identifier.

### Date
YYYY-MM-DD.

### Decision
A clear and concise description of what was decided.

### Rationale
Why this decision was made.

### Impact
What this decision affects or changes.

### Affected Files
List of documents or areas impacted by this decision.
