# Context Packet
This document captures the current working mental model of the project.
It reflects approved decisions and the present focus only.

## Current Objective
- The primary objective the project is currently focused on.
- This objective must align with the active development stage.
- Only one objective may exist at a time.

## Active Constraints
- Constraints currently shaping decisions and scope.
- Constraints may be technical, temporal, financial, or conceptual.
- Constraints must originate from logged decisions.

## Current Assumptions
- Assumptions currently treated as true.
- Assumptions are provisional and may change.
- Assumptions must not contradict logged decisions.

## Open Questions
- Questions that block progress or require clarification.
- Questions must be answerable.
- Unanswered questions may prevent further action.

## Current Focus
- What the team or LLM should focus on right now.
- Focus must be actionable.
- Focus must not imply unapproved decisions.
